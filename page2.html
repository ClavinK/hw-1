A Short Explanation of my Hobbies
Gaming
- Some of my favorite games are single player open world RPG's including Elden Ring, Final Fantasy 7, Skyrim, Red Dead Redepemtion 2 and more.
My passion for video games came from my oldest cousin who showed me his games when I was really young so we both share the same hobby to this day.
Swimming
- Swimming is my favorite form of cardio and is just overall a fun thing to do especially in the summer.
I may not be the greatest swimmer, but when the opportunity arises I will swim laps in a pool or go as far as I can possibly go in the ocean and swim.
Cooking
- Cooking was actually something I was considering doing as a career before I made a final decision on pursuing Computer Science.
Watching my mom cook as well as other famous chefs like Gordon Ramsay were my inspirations to learn more about cooking.
Cosplay
- Only recently I started doing cosplays for Conventions. It is such a cool experience but also very expensive.
The idea of dressing up as your favorite character from any media is such an interesting experience. I plan to do more cosplays and attend more conventions in the future.
Hiking
- Hiking for me is such a relaxing experience, it lets me clear my head and enjoy the scenery if there is any.
It definitely is one of my go to methods of relieving stress after school or work.
